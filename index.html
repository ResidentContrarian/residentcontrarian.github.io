I'm the Resident Contrarian, and I write on the internet.

My justified use case is one where you need both of two specific things:

1) A writer with excellent prose. If you are looking for me, chances are good that you have an education and can write reasonably well. If you need me, it's because you need or want someone who writes even better than that; you need someone who can get your audience to feel, understand, and engage with things better than conventional-good writing allows.

2) A writer who understands things well and quickly. You are communicating information about complex products or subjects, and need someone who can talk about that subject matter with a minimal amount of help.
